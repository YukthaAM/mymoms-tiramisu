Our Story

A Mother's Legacy of Love

My Mom's Dessert began in our family kitchen, where my mother perfected her recipes over decades — each one crafted with patience, the finest ingredients, and a whole lot of love. What started as a family tradition has now become Bangalore's most sought-after homemade dessert destination.

Every dessert we make is prepared fresh, in small batches, using only the highest quality ingredients — no shortcuts, no preservatives, just pure homemade goodness made exactly the way mom intended.
