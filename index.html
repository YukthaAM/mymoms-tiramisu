Our Story

Made just like
We used to make it

Every jar of our tiramisu begins in a home kitchen, not a factory. Layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, silky mascarpone cream, and a generous dusting of cocoa lovingly made for every family occasion.

What started as a passion has grown into something we want to share with everyone. Because the best desserts aren't made at scale — they're made with patience, the finest ingredients, and a whole lot of love.

Every order is freshly prepared to order. No preservatives. No alcohol. And eggless. Just the real thing.
